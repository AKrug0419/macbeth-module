Module Eight:
The Dystopian
Film Research Essay

Researching and writing a 700-word analysis of how dystopian characteristics function within a film — supported by three secondary sources and documented in MLA format.

Blade Runner 2049 (Alcon Entertainment)

MOV·01

About This Module

This module builds directly on the work you did in Module Seven. Having studied the key characteristics of dystopian societies, the varieties of dystopian control, and the traits of the dystopian protagonist, you will now apply those concepts to a focused research essay on a single film of your choice.

Over the next two weeks you will select a film from the college's streaming library, locate three secondary sources (one from an academic database, two from the open web), and write a four-paragraph, 700-word analysis of how two dystopian characteristics function within the film. Along the way, you'll practice the full research workflow: searching, evaluating, summarizing, paraphrasing, quoting, and documenting.

The Essay at a Glance

700 words minimum · 4 paragraphs (introduction, two body paragraphs, conclusion) · 3 secondary sources (1 database article, 2 web sources) · MLA format · Children of Men, Pan's Labyrinth, or The Matrix as the primary source.

MOV·04

Learning Activities

This module includes three types of learning activities. They run in parallel across the two weeks — as you view your chosen film, you will also be reading about research and drafting your essay.

Critical Reading
  • Internet research strategy and the SIFT source-evaluation method
  • AI and academic research: hallucination, verification, and integrity
  • MLA documentation: film as primary source, secondary source patterns, paraphrase, summary, and Works Cited
Critical Viewing
  • Children of Men, Pan's Labyrinth, or The Matrix — streamed independently through the college's Swank library
Drafting & Revising
  • The Writing Process: prewriting and planning
  • The Writing Process: drafting and revising
  • The Writing Process: editing and proofreading
