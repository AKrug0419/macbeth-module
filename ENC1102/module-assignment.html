ASN·02

Reference Lists

Use the three lists below to select the dystopian characteristics you will analyze in your essay. You will choose a total of three items from across all three lists.

List A Characteristics of a Dystopian Society
  • ◆Propaganda is used to control the citizens of society.
  • ◆Information, independent thought, and freedom are restricted.
  • ◆A figurehead or concept is worshipped by the citizens of the society.
  • ◆Citizens are perceived to be under constant surveillance.
  • ◆Citizens have a fear of the outside world.
  • ◆Citizens live in a dehumanized state — the "loss of humanity."
  • ◆The natural world is banished and distrusted.
  • ◆Citizens conform to uniform expectations. Individuality and dissent are discouraged.
  • ◆The society is an illusion of a perfect utopian world.
List B Types of Dystopian Controls

Most dystopian works present a world in which oppressive societal control is maintained through one or more of the following:

  • ◆Corporate control — One or more large corporations control society through products, advertising, and/or the media.
  • ◆Bureaucratic control — Society is controlled by a mindless bureaucracy through red tape, relentless regulations, and incompetent government.
  • ◆Technological control — Society is controlled by technology — through computers, robots, and/or scientific means.
  • ◆Philosophical/religious control — Society is controlled by philosophical or religious ideology, often enforced through a dictatorship or theocracy.
List C The Dystopian Protagonist
  • ◆Often feels trapped and is struggling to escape.
  • ◆Questions the existing social and political order.
  • ◆Believes or feels that something is terribly wrong with the society in which he or she lives.
  • ◆Helps the audience recognize the negative aspects of the dystopian world through his or her perspective.
ASN·03

Assignment Instructions

Instead of specific questions as in previous assignments, you're being asked to do something requiring a slightly higher level of critical thinking. Follow the three steps below.

Step One Select Your Three Items

Select a total of three items from the lists above — List A: Characteristics of a Dystopian Society, List B: Types of Dystopian Controls, and List C: The Dystopian Protagonist — that you feel are relevant to the film you chose to watch.

The three items you select can all come from the same list, or you can select one from each list, or two from one list and one from another — it doesn't matter. Focus on selecting items you believe are present and relevant to your film.

Step Two Write Your Three Paragraphs

Write three total paragraphs discussing how the three dystopian characteristics you selected are present in your film. Write one paragraph for each of the three characteristics you chose.

Step Three Submit Your Work

When done, upload your paragraphs just as you did for all previous study questions this semester.

Length 350–400 words total (all three paragraphs combined)
