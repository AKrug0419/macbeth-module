Module Overview

What We’re Studying This Module

In this module, we’ll be studying the literary genre known as poetry. In addition, we’ll be focusing on two central literary elements that poets rely on to create meaning: imagery and figurative language.

Keep in Mind

Poetry works differently from other kinds of writing. Rather than stating ideas directly, poets render them in concrete images and figurative comparisons—and trust readers to interpret what those images suggest. The skills you develop in this module will help you read any poem more carefully and confidently.
